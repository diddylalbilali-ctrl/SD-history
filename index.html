Slayers of Destiny – Clan History

Founded by Light, Slayers of Destiny (SD) began as a single, unified clan built on strength, loyalty, and progression. In its early days, the clan steadily grew to 40 members, forming a tight-knit community.

As the community expanded, internal differences emerged, leading to a split into two factions: PO and GA. Initially allied, tensions grew, and the factions eventually became rivals.

Over time, both PO and GA declined and ultimately disbanded, marking the end of an era.

From the ashes, one former owner created VT, carrying on GA's legacy and influence.

Recognizing the strength of their original vision, the former leaders reunited to rebuild Slayers of Destiny, stronger, more unified, and built to last.

– Led by Destiny
